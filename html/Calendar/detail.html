Youth Summer Hangout

An evening of fun, games, and fellowship for all high school students!

Back to All Events

About This Event

Get ready for an unforgettable evening designed just for high school students! Our Annual Youth Summer Hangout is the perfect opportunity to connect with friends, make new ones, and enjoy a vibrant atmosphere filled with fun and fellowship.

We'll have a range of exciting activities including:

  • Outdoor **games and sports** (volleyball, frisbee, giant Jenga)
  • Delicious **snacks and refreshments** (pizza, soda, fruit)
  • A **bonfire** with s'mores (weather permitting)
  • Engaging **group discussions** on topics relevant to youth faith
  • Live **music and worship** session
  • Opportunities to simply **relax and chat** with peers and youth leaders

This event is open to all high school students (ages 14-18), whether you're a regular at our church or just looking for a cool place to hang out. We aim to provide a safe, welcoming, and inspiring environment where youth can grow in their faith and build lasting friendships. Don't miss out on the highlight of the summer!

Important Notes:

  • What to Bring Comfortable clothes for outdoor activities, a water bottle, and a friend!
  • Safety Adult supervision will be provided at all times. First aid will be available on site.
  • Transportation Please arrange your own transportation to and from the church.
  • Dietary Restrictions If you have any allergies or dietary needs, please inform us on the registration form.
